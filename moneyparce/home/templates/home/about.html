About MoneyParce

MoneyParce is a web application designed to help users efficiently track their income and expenses, analyze spending patterns, and receive personalized financial insights. By integrating banking data with AI-driven analysis and intuitive visualizations, MoneyParce empowers users to take control of their finances and make smarter, informed decisions about their money.

With features like automatic expense tracking via the Plaid API, budgeting tools, and interactive AI-driven recommendations, MoneyParce simplifies money management and helps users make smarter financial choices. The platform also offers detailed financial reports and visualizations that make it easy to understand spending habits and adjust behaviors for better financial health.
